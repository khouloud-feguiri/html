the specialties

The dental clinic offers a range of complete services in dentistry: Implant, whitening, veneer, dental care

Dental Implants

A dental implant is an artificial root, a kind of metal screw (titanium or zirconia), implanted in the alveolar bone to replace missing teeth.

Dental Veneer

The dental facet is a shell which makes it possible to cover the visible face of the anterior teeth to modify their color, their shape, their alignment... They are placed when the teeth are stained, discolored, or worn, to hide the imperfections of 'a smile.

Teeth whitening

Dental whitening is aesthetic intervention that is increasingly in demand. It consists of finding the original shade of your teeth or a lighter shade than the current one using carbamide peroxide or hydrogen peroxide.
